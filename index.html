More Than a Car. It's a Legacy.

Unleashing Performance

BMW and Kith collaborate for the third chapter of their partnership, showcasing three iconic vehicles that bridge heritage and innovation: the restored 1981 BMW M1 E26 by Ronnie Fieg, the 2024 BMW XM Kith Concept and the limited-production 2025 BMW XM by Kith, which is the purchasable offer for the 2024 BMW XM Kith Concept.

Ronnie Fieg, the Founder, CEO & Creative Director of Kith is a renowned designer celebrated for blending fashion, art, and culture. As a lifelong BMW enthusiast, he brings his visionary design approach to this collaboration.

Fieg worked together with a skilled team to reimagine the BMW M1, a 1970s sports car icon. The vehicle has been meticulously restored including leather sports seats featuring embossed Kith logos. Its exterior is finished in the rare Techno Violet color, a nod to the BMW design legacy from the 1990s.

The 2025 BMW XM by Kith offers cutting-edge performance with an M TwinPower V8 engine delivering 748 HP and a 0-100 km/h acceleration time of just 3.8 seconds. Its bespoke interior includes BMW Individual Black Merino Leather, Kith monograms, and illuminated headrest logos. Available in BMW Individual Frozen Techno Violet metallic and BMW Individual Frozen Black metallic, this model is limited to just 47 units worldwide.

Experience the magic:

Halloween season

with BMW

From end of October, BMW customers in selected markets can enjoy in their vehicle a special festive animation video to celebrate Halloween.

With the Festive App, BMW sends animated videos accompanied with festive sound and atmospheric ambient lightning to the customer’s vehicles: this year BMW is surprising its customers in their BMWs with a festive animation for Halloween. While watching the animation video, customers can immerse in the charm of funny shadow plays, spooky characters, and a heart-warming new friendship.

Customers will receive a notification in their control display once the vehicle has been started, signaling the approaching Halloween celebration. By clicking the notification, the BMW “Happy Halloween” animation video will start and is available until the beginning of November. Customers can replay the animation as often as liked during the festive season by clicking on the app icon in the BMW all apps menu.

To display the festive animations, the vehicles need to have the appropriate optional equipment and a BMW iDrive with Operating System 7, 8, 8.5 or 9, as well as a production date after 07/2020.
